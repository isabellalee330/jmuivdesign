About JMU IV

Our desire, as we hope is yours, is to see the name of Jesus magnified and exalted on our campus, in the lives of our lost friends, and ultimately around the globe.

  • InterVarsity is more than an organization. We don't want to be another program or function for people to attend.
  • We want to be a community that builds strong disciples of Christ who engage the world with the life, message, and worth of Jesus Christ.
  • JMU doesn't need more Christian meetings, clever ideas, or catchy programs. Our campus needs God.

Get Involved

Small groups in InterVarsity are more than just Bible studies. They are witnessing communities for Christ. With God at the center, a small group grows into an established community that delves into the values of the word, worship and witness.

  • Meet once a week for fellowship and Bible study.
  • Are led by two trained and committed co-leaders.
  • Build community through trips, hangouts, games, tailgates, service and much more.
